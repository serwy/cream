Getting Started
CREAM is an acronym for Cognitive Reliability and Error Analysis Method. It is a tool for analysis errors that already happened and for predicting errors that may happen.
Step-by-Step
1) Choose your analysis direction
2) Define the Common Performance Conditions
3) Proceed with the analysis
Abreviations
DL - direct link
IL - indirect link
SC - specific consequent
SA - specific antecedent
GA - general antecedent
GC - general consequent
